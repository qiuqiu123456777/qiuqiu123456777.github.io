Cycling

Recently, having known that our college has a group which organizes a series of riding activities, I decided to join in the club. The reason why I have a passion for cycling is during the pandemic when I felt stressed and lonely I took a ride in the national park near my home by accident. It proved that it's quite a relief. As a result, cycling grew to me.

In the first semester, we saw the sunrise in the park next to our school, viewed the boardered and crystal-like lake after riding about 45 kilometers, had lunch together to exchange our emotions and feelings, and so on. I felt no regret that I joined the club, and I really enjoyed the cycling.
